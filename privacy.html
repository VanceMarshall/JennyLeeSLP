Privacy Policy

Jenny Lee LLC  ·  Last updated: March 2026

This policy explains what information Jenny Lee SLP collects through the contact form on this website, how it is used, and what it is not used for.

What we collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Your name (parent or guardian)
  • Your child's name and age
  • Your phone number and email address
  • Your preferred contact method
  • Any message or details you include in the free-text field

How we use it

The information you submit is used solely to respond to your inquiry and determine whether Jenny Lee SLP can help your family. It is not used for marketing, sold to third parties, or shared with anyone outside of Jenny Lee LLC.

Important notice about this form and HIPAA

This contact form is for general inquiries only. It is not a HIPAA-secure channel. Please do not submit protected health information (PHI), medical records, or other sensitive health details through this form.

A formal patient relationship, including any applicable HIPAA protections, begins only after you have been officially accepted as a patient and completed the required intake paperwork. Questions about privacy in the context of care can be directed to admin@jennyleeslp.com.

Third-party services

Form submissions are processed through Web3Forms, which delivers your message to Jenny's inbox. Web3Forms does not store form submissions after delivery. This website also uses Google Fonts, loaded directly from Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's standard practices.

Data retention

Inquiry emails received through this form are retained in Jenny's inbox and deleted when no longer needed. No separate database of contact form submissions is maintained.

Your rights

You may request that we delete any information you submitted through this form by emailing admin@jennyleeslp.com.
